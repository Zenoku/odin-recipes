Beef Stew

Description

An easy to make recipe for any amount of people

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef stew meat
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flower
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Place meat in slow cooker
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the flour, salt and pepper
  3. Pour the stuff from the small bowl over the meat
  4. Stir to coat meat with flour mixture
  5. Stir in the garlic, bay leaf, paprika, Worchestershire sauce, beef broth, potatoes, carrots, and celery
  6. Cover and cook on low setting for 10-12 hours or on high setting for 4-6 hours
